WHAT WE HELP WITH

Simple parent support before, during, and after the meeting.

This page is for education advocacy and meeting support. It is not a law firm page and it is not a clinical ABA page. The goal is simple: help parents understand school options, documents, and next steps.

Meeting prep

Get ready for an IEP, CPSE, CSE, reevaluation, or annual review meeting with clear notes and a question list.

Document review

We help you organize notices, evaluations, progress reports, and school paperwork into plain language.

Parent advocacy

We can join a meeting with you, help you track what is being discussed, and make the process feel less overwhelming.

Local next steps

Need more support after the meeting? We help families understand what to ask next and where to go from here.

WHO THIS IS FOR

For families who want support, not more confusion.

Good fit
